My Top 5 Albums of All Time

  1. The Dark Side of the Moon - Pink Floyd (1973)

    Released: March 1, 1973
    Label: Harvest Records (UK) / Capitol Records (US)
    Billboard 200 Peak: #1
    Weeks on Billboard 200: 741 (all-time record)
    Estimated Sales: 45 million copies worldwide
    [1]

  2. Revolver - The Beatles (1966)

    Released: August 5, 1966
    Label: Parlophone (UK) / Capitol Records (US)
    Billboard 200 Peak: #1
    UK Albums Chart Peak: #1
    [2]

  3. Wildflowers - Tom Petty (1994)

    Released: November 1, 1994
    Label: Warner Bros. Records
    Billboard 200 Peak: #8
    RIAA Certification: 2× Platinum
    [3]

  4. Ten - Pearl Jam (1991)

    Released: August 27, 1991
    Label: Epic Records
    Billboard 200 Peak: #2
    RIAA Certification: 13× Platinum
    Weeks on Billboard 200: 261
    [4]

  5. The Black Parade - My Chemical Romance (2006)

    Released: October 23, 2006
    Label: Reprise Records
    Billboard 200 Peak: #2
    RIAA Certification: 3× Platinum
    [5]
